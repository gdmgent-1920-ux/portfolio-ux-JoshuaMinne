WE ARE VINTAGEOUS

Buying vintage, is buying a unique piece. As our lights are 40, 50, 60 years old, they have corresponding traces of use. It’s that vintage feel, of a quality piece that never got out of style as the decades passed by.

Any old wiring is replaced for new wiring to offer you a safe lamp. However: we cannot guarantee that our lights will meet todays stricter safety standards, because they were designed decades ago. We recommend to outsource the installation of your light to a professional electrician.
